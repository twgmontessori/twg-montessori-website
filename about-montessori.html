What Is Montessori Education?

Montessori education was developed by Dr. Maria Montessori more than one hundred years ago. Instead of asking every child to learn in exactly the same way, Montessori recognizes that each child develops at their own pace. Children learn through carefully prepared environments, hands-on materials, independent exploration, and guidance from trained Montessori educators. Today, Montessori schools can be found throughout the world and are respected for helping children become confident, curious, independent, and lifelong learners.
