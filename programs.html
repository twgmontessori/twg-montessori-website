Casa · Ages 2.5–5

A full day of Montessori learning, care, and belonging.

Together We Grow offers a full-day Montessori Casa program for children ages 2.5 to 5 in Vancouver. Our mixed-age classrooms provide children with the time, space, and guidance they need to develop concentration, independence, confidence, and a genuine love of learning.

Each day includes an uninterrupted Montessori work cycle, outdoor exploration, meals, rest or quiet experiences, community gatherings, and thoughtfully selected enrichment. These parts of the day work together to support the whole child rather than treating learning, care, movement, and relationships as separate experiences.

The Three-Year Casa Journey

Children grow from newcomers into confident classroom leaders.

The Montessori Casa experience is designed as a multi-year journey. By remaining in a familiar prepared environment, children build upon what they have already learned and gradually take on new levels of independence, responsibility, and leadership.

Year One

Building the Foundation

Children develop independence, concentration, and confidence through purposeful hands-on work. During the first year, they build routines, care for themselves and their environment, and begin exploring every area of the Montessori curriculum.

Year Two

Growing with Confidence

As children gain experience, they deepen their curiosity, strengthen problem-solving skills, and work with increasing independence. Their confidence grows through meaningful choices and self-directed learning.

Year Three

Leading with Kindness

Older children naturally mentor younger classmates, developing leadership, empathy, and responsibility while reinforcing their own knowledge. This mixed-age community is one of Montessori's greatest strengths.

The Power of Growing Together

Every child has the opportunity to experience different roles: the newcomer who observes, the explorer who discovers, and the leader who supports others. This progression develops not only knowledge and skills, but empathy, patience, confidence, and character.

The Montessori Curriculum

A Day at Together We Grow

A calm and balanced daily rhythm.

Our two Montessori Casa classrooms follow staggered daily schedules so each group can enjoy dedicated outdoor time, focused classroom experiences, and smooth transitions.

The rhythm remains consistent enough to help children feel secure, while educators remain responsive to individual needs, developmental readiness, weather, and the natural life of the classroom.

7:30 – 8:30 AM
Optional Early Care Arrival, handwashing, breakfast, and a gentle beginning to the day.
8:00 – 9:00 AM
Staggered Classroom Arrival Warm greetings, family transitions, classroom welcome, stories, songs, or the beginning of Montessori work.
Morning
Montessori Work Cycle and Outdoor Exploration Individual and small-group lessons, independent work, snack, classroom care, and dedicated outdoor time scheduled separately for each class.
12:00 – 12:45 PM
Lunch and Classroom Community Handwashing, table preparation, lunch, conversation, and participation in caring for the shared environment.
12:45 – 1:45 PM
Rest or Quiet Experiences Children who sleep are supported in a calm rest environment. Children who do not nap are offered quiet play, books, stories, or peaceful activities.
Afternoon
Montessori Learning, Outdoor Play, and Enrichment The two classrooms rotate between outdoor experiences, Montessori work, snack, circle gatherings, and thoughtfully scheduled enrichment.
4:30 – 5:00 PM
Regular Dismissal Children transition home through staggered classroom dismissal procedures.
5:00 – 6:00 PM
Optional Late Care A relaxed end to the day with stories, puzzles, LEGO, art, and quiet social experiences.
Exact classroom timing may vary slightly because the Dolphin and Beluga classes use staggered schedules for arrival, outdoor play, enrichment, and dismissal. This allows each group to move through the day safely and with greater space, attention, and continuity.

Supporting the Whole Child

Learning, movement, rest, and relationships belong together.

A meaningful early childhood program must support more than academic development. Our daily environment responds to children’s physical, emotional, social, and developmental needs with consistency, flexibility, and care.

☀️

Daily Outdoor Learning

Dedicated outdoor periods offer opportunities for movement, nature exploration, imaginative play, gross motor development, cooperation, and joyful physical activity.

🌙

Rest and Quiet Time

Children who need sleep are supported in resting comfortably. Children who do not nap participate in peaceful alternatives such as books, stories, quiet play, and calm individual work.

🤝

Responsive Individual Support

Educators consider each child’s readiness, transitions, regulation, family context, and developmental needs when providing guidance and adapting daily experiences.

Beyond the Montessori Work Cycle

Enrichment that complements our Montessori foundation.

Enrichment experiences are thoughtfully scheduled so they extend creativity, movement, communication, and discovery without replacing or unnecessarily interrupting the uninterrupted Montessori work cycle.

🎨

Creative Arts

Art, music, storytelling, and creative expression nurture imagination, communication, and emotional development.

🤸

Movement and Wellness

Yoga, RBLM Motor Lab, and gross motor activities support coordination, balance, body awareness, and physical confidence.

🤖

STEM Discovery

Robotics, LEGO experiences, and hands-on problem solving introduce logical thinking, collaboration, design, and experimentation.

🌍

Language and Culture

French, music, cultural learning, and community experiences help children become curious, respectful, and connected to the wider world.

Extended Care

Additional flexibility for working families.

Optional extended-care services support families who require an earlier beginning or later end to their child’s school day. These services are selected during registration and scheduled according to family needs and enrollment.

Optional Early Care

Early care is available from 7:30 to 8:30 AM. Children are welcomed into a calm supervised environment with time for arrival, handwashing, breakfast, and a gradual transition into the school day.

Optional Late Care

Late care is available from 5:00 to 6:00 PM. Children enjoy a relaxed closing rhythm that may include stories, LEGO, puzzles, art, conversation, and quiet social activities.
